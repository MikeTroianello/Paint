PAINT!

Paint bubbles are coming to ruin your canvas! Shoot paint at them to combine colors, and pop them over their corresponding tiles.

EXAMPLE: Shoot a Blue bubble with Yellow paint over a Green square.

If you shoot the wrong color combination, the tile turns to sludge and will no longer award you points. Shoot carefully.

SHOOT
